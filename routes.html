📍 Itinerary for 2-Day Trip

Day 1: Udayagiri & Khandagiri Caves → ISKCON Temple

⏰ Timings: 9am - 5pm
The historic caves built for Jain monks, offering meditation spots and intricate carvings.

ISKCON Temple

⏰ Timings: 8am - 9pm
Built in 1991, this temple is an alternative to Puri’s Jagannath Temple, welcoming all devotees.

Day 2: Lingaraj Temple → Mukteshwar Temple

⏰ Timings: 6:30am - 9pm
Dedicated to Lord Shiva, Lingaraj Temple is one of the oldest temples in Bhubaneswar.

📍 Itinerary for 3-Day Trip

Day 1: Jagannath Temple → Golden Beach

⏰ Timings: 6am - 9pm
One of the Char Dham pilgrimage sites, famous for Rath Yatra.

Day 2: Konark Sun Temple → Mukteshwar Temple

⏰ Timings: 6am - 8pm
A 13th-century Sun temple, masterpiece of Eastern Ganga dynasty.

Day 3: Chandipur Beach

⏰ Timings: 8am - 9pm
Known for its receding waters and proximity to DRDO’s test range.
